Home Schedule Guests Tickets About

Event Name: BurtonFest
Event Type: Film festival
Event Theme: Celebration of Tim Burton Movies
Target Audience: Fans of Tim Burton movies, film enthusiasts, and general audiences.
Event Objectives: To showcase the works of Tim Burton and celebrate his unique cinematic style. To create an immersive and engaging experience for attendees.
Other Details: The festival will feature screenings of Tim Burton movies, panels and discussions with industry experts and special guests, and interactive exhibits.

The actors and actresses from Tim Burton's greatest films will be giving answering questions signing autographs. There will also be a screening of Cropse Bride, Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, and Beetlejuice Screening of his up coming movies Wonka, Beetlejuice 2, and a surprise movie will be shown. Get ready to see a never before seen movie.

To celebrate the unique cinematic style of Tim Burton and provide an immersive experience for attendees.

Ticket prices: $20 all ages 2 and under free
